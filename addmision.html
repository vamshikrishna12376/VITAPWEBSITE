Academic Programs at VIT-AP University

VIT-AP offers a wide range of distinctive education programs through its Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Ph.D. Programmes. The teaching-learning process at the institute prepares students for the future, involving them in an active process of applied learning with the guidance of experienced faculty.

Our programs are designed to enable students to channel their talent and intellect to contribute to the development and sustainability of society. With an emphasis on innovation and applying technology to improve life, we offer the following programs at various levels:

  • B.Tech.
  • BBA
  • LAW
  • B.A. - M.A.
  • B.Sc.
  • M.Sc.
  • B.Com.
  • M.TECH.
  • International Programs
  • Ph.D.

Application Process

Our application process is straightforward and designed to help you get started on your academic journey. To apply for any of our programs, please visit our official website and follow the application instructions provided.

Fees & Scholarships

We offer a variety of financial aid options and scholarships to eligible students. To learn more about the fees for your chosen program and available scholarships, please visit our Financial Aid and Scholarships page on our website.
